About me

So now here we are. I like exploring new things. Two of my many hobbies are travelling and eating with family and friends.

I did a computer science research there and met lots of people from a different culture. I believe that any kinds of knowledge would take me one step further in my life. So that's probably why I embrace all the new things happening in my life.

Lastly, this project is a bit challenging but fun. It's challenging because I didn't have any experience doing web design like this, expecially from scratch. The start was a bit rough but I started to taste the fun part of it once I have some components set up. In the future, I want to work in a game development company, probably in a project management team. I want to bridge and gap between technology and business.
